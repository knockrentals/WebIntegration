Step 2: Setup your iframe integration

  1. Get your company identifier! You can find it at your company profile page.
  2. Add an iframe anywhere on your page with our URL template. Here's an example:
    3. <iframe src="https://knockrentals.com/company/YOUR_COMPANY_ID_HERE?isExternal=true&companyName=YOUR_COMPANY_ID_HERE"></iframe>

That's it, you're done!
